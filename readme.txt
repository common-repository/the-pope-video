Show the oficial The Pope Video of the Vatican, with the prayer intention of Pope Francis. Always up-to-date and easy to install.

== Description ==

The oficial plugin of The Pope Video, the monthly video of Pope Francis with his prayer intention confided to the Pope’s Worldwide Prayer Network. It will permit you to have on your website the latest published video automatically without needing to worry about downloading it each month. The installation is very simple.


= Characteristics =

Now you can automatically have the latest edition of The Pope Video always up-to-date on your website and help the Pope to spread to your community his monthly prayer intentions for the challenges of humanity and of the mission of the Church.

* Super easy to configure
  With the simple step of inserting a shortcode The Pope Video will embed on your page, in the place you desire.

* It ncludes a reproduction list.
  The plugin of The Pope Video will reproduce the latest published video and then will continue reproducing the videos of future months.

* Available in 5 languages
  You can install the plugin in English, Spanish, Italian, French, and Portuguese.

* Autoplay
  The Pope Video Plugin does not use autoplay, so that your visitors can navigate to your site without interruptions and reproduce the videos that you desire.


= Why do you need this plugin? =

Installing The Pope Video Plugin on your site has many advantages.

* Official content
  Your visitors are going to be able to make use of the oficial content especially confided by Pope Francis, directly from the Pope’s Worldwide Prayer Network.

* You will save time.
  You will no longer need to configure The Pope Video each month on your page. It will automatically appear the day of the oficial launch.

* You will refresh the image of your site.
  Through a miniature of The Pope Video, you will not only have one video, you will have all of them! The Pope Video Plugin automatically reproduces the complete archive of The Pope Video in the language that you desire. This includes 5 years of current themes for humanity and the mission of the Church.
